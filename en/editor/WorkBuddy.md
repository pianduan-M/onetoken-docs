#### Overview

WorkBuddy supports the integration of third-party custom models, allowing you to choose different Large Language Models (LLMs) to power your conversations based on your specific needs. This article provides a detailed guide on how to configure the **OneToken** platform.

1. Click on Model Selection, then click on Configure Custom Model.

<img src="/images/workBuddy/1_en.png" />

<img src="/images/workBuddy/2_en.png" />

2. Click **Add Model**, scroll to the bottom of the provider list, and select **Custom**.

<img src="/images/workBuddy/3_en.png" />

<img src="/images/workBuddy/4_en.png" />

3. Fill in the model information:
   - **API Endpoint:** Enter `[https://onetoken.one/v1](https://onetoken.one/v1)`
   - **API Key:** Use the API Key obtained from the [OneToken Platform](https://onetoken.one/console/token).
   - **Model Name:** Enter a supported model name from the [Model List](https://onetoken.one/pricing).
   - **Advanced Configuration:** Configure based on the capabilities of your chosen model.
     Once completed, click **Save**.

<img src="/images/workBuddy/5_en.png" />

4. Return to the main window, select the custom model you just added, and you are ready to go!

<img src="/images/workBuddy/6_en.png" />
